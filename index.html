Who

Am I?

I am a Front-end enthusiast who started the journey of web development in November of 2021. It's then when I stumbled upon a YouTube video of the guy that was making a portfolio website for himself using HTML and CSS. The website he created was so beautiful that I thought to myself: I want to do the same! I followed the guy's tutorial and after a couple of weeks I had my own Website. From that moment I knew that creating web pages & applications is what I really want to do in my life.

Fast forward to 2023 and I taught myself HTML, CSS (including preprocessor SASS) and JavaScript. I've also worked with frameworks like Angular and React. Although at the moment I'm yet to obtain a commercial experience as a developer I am constantly learning and improving my skills every single day. I'm also the type of person that will always do everything that can be done to find a solution to a problem. And I think that's really important in this field. “Hard work beats talent when talent doesn't work hard” -Tim Notke
